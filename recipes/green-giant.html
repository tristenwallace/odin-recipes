Green Giant

Bryan Johnson has a “Green Giant” drink upon waking up which consists of Spermidine, Amino complex, Creatine, Collagen, Cocoa, and Ceylon Cinnamon. These Ingredients all have proported benefits on health longevity. I recommend batch mixing a month of the following recipe and storing in a vaccuum container for easy morning access.

Ingredients

  • 20 oz of water
  • Spermidine, 2 Tbsp chlorella powder, yielding 13.5mg spermidine*
  • Amino Complex (lemon) 7.6 g
  • Creatine 2.5 g
  • Collagen Peptides 20 g
  • Cocoa Flavanols 500 mg
  • Ceylon Cinnamon 1 tsp

Instructions (Monthly Batch)

  1. Place very large mixing bowl (like 2-3 ft. diameter) on scale.
  2. Multiply each ingredients amount by 30 and deposit into bowl.
  3. Use a whisk to mix until homogenous.
  4. Transfer to vaccuum food storage
  5. Clean up: it gets a bit dusty.
