Cottage Cheese Eggs

Description:

These cottage cheese scrambled eggs are a delicious and nutritious breakfast option. Packed with protein, sautéed vegetables, and flax seeds, they provide a balanced and energizing start to your day.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup cottage cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, finely chopped (adjust to your spice preference)
  • 1 tablespoon ground flax seed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add a little cooking oil or cooking spray to the skillet.
  3. Sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until they become translucent and fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped spinach and cook until it wilts and reduces in volume.
  5. Stir in the diced tomato and jalapeno pepper. Cook for a few minutes until the tomatoes start to soften.
  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and cottage cheese until well combined.
  7. Pour the egg and cottage cheese mixture into the skillet with the sautéed vegetables.
  8. Stir the mixture continuously, allowing the eggs to cook and scramble. This should take about 3-5 minutes until the eggs are fully cooked but still moist.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Sprinkle the ground flax seed over the scrambled eggs and stir to incorporate.
  11. Remove from heat and serve hot.

Enjoy your healthy and protein-packed cottage cheese scrambled eggs!
